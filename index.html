About Us

Seotech was founded in 2012 by two SEO enthusiasts who believed that search optimization and strategic content could transform businesses of all sizes. Starting in a home office with just one client, we focused on mastering organic search, technical SEO, and content marketing. By 2015, our results-driven approach had earned us a growing roster of clients across e‑commerce, technology, and professional services. In 2018, we expanded our service offerings to include paid search, social media advertising, and full-funnel analytics. Today, Seotech proudly serves over 150 clients worldwide, delivering measurable growth and long-term value.

Our Mission. We empower brands to achieve their highest potential online by combining data‑backed SEO, creative content, and performance marketing. Our goal is to drive sustainable growth, increase visibility, and build authentic connections between businesses and their audiences.

Partner with Seotech and let our expertise in search, content, and performance marketing unlock the next level of growth for your brand.
